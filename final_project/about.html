The Cook Behind The Book

Hello, my name is Olivia!

Cooking has always been one of my go-to ways of expressing affection and gratitude. One of my favorite feelings is seeing peoples' faces light up when they taste something I put my time and effort into.

That's why I love making Vietnamese and Chinese dishes. Not only do they remind me of my heritage and the wonderful family dinners I enjoyed as a kid, they are meticulous and take a lot of care to execute.

I understand that I am not able to cook for everyone in the world, but that shouldn't prevent people from trying my recipes. That's why I've put together this small internet archive to share some of my favorite dishes. I hope you enjoy them!
