Bun Rieu

Bun Rieu, a Vietnamese soup, is a culinary masterpiece that captivates the palate with its intricate blend of savory, sweet, and umami notes. The heart of this dish lies in its rich broth, a harmonious combination of fresh tomatoes, crab paste, and aromatic herbs. The soup features delicate rice vermicelli noodles, creating a satisfyingly chewy texture, and is generously adorned with succulent crab or shrimp, tender pork, and earthy tofu. Garnished with fresh herbs, a squeeze of lime, and a sprinkle of chili, each bowl of Bun Rieu is a sensory journey through the vibrant flavors of Vietnam, telling a story of tradition and culinary artistry.

Bun Rieu is one of my personal favorites. It's quick, easy, and a must try.

Overnight Focaccia

Focaccia, an Italian delight, is a culinary marvel that elevates the art of bread. This oven-baked flatbread boasts a golden, olive oil-infused crust, and its pillowy interior is adorned with a symphony of flavors. Adorned with tomatoes, rosemary, and coarse salt, each bite of focaccia is a delightful balance of crispy and tender textures, creating a sensory experience that captures the essence of Mediterranean cuisine.

This is an adaption of a recipe by LaceBakes. The overnight feature make this recipe extremely easy to prep ahead for a nice brunch!

Thit Kho Truong

Thit Kho Truong, a Vietnamese comfort dish, invites you to savor the rich and nuanced flavors of caramelized pork belly. Slow-cooked to perfection, the succulent pork is bathed in a savory-sweet sauce, infused with the warmth of fish sauce, coconut water, and a hint of black pepper. The result is a tender and flavorful masterpiece, often enjoyed with steamed rice, creating a comforting and deeply satisfying meal.

This dish is a household favorite. While it takes a bit of time to prepare, it is incredibly easy and worth the effort.

Garlic & Chive Twist Buns

Garlic and Chive Twist Buns are a delightful fusion of flavors and textures. These whimsical creations feature soft, twisted buns infused with aromatic garlic and fresh chives. With each bite, you experience the satisfying pull-apart texture of the bread, complemented by the savory notes of garlic and the mild onion-like essence of chives. Perfect as a standalone snack or alongside a meal, these twist buns are a delicious testament to the art of baking.

These buns are the perfect shareable. They're beautiful and are undeniably a crowd pleaser
