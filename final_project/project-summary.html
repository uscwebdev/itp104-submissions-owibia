Final Project Summary | Olivia Dien | ITP104

Topic:

The topic of my project is an online cookbook.

Purpose:

The purpose of my project is to create a cookbook of my favorite recipes that I can easily share with others. One of the reasons I am so passionate about cooking is the universal joy it can bring to others whether they are the chef or the consumer.

Extras:

  1. I have a submission page where users can submit new recipe ideas/revisions.
  2. I have included transformations on the recipe page to scale up the recipe descriptions when hovered on.
  3. I used the JavaScript plugin Fade-In-Scroll on the front page.
